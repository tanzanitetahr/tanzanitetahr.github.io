The text is complemented by many exercises that serve to check the comprehension of the text treat further examples or give an outlook on further results. The volume at hand is an introduction to schemes. To get startet it requires only basic knowledge in abstract algebra and topology. Essential facts from commutative algebra are assembled in an appendix. It will be complemented by a second volume on the cohomology of schemes.
